Who We Are

Bringing God's Healing to
Every Corner of Liberia

Zion Healing and Deliverance Ministries International (ZHDMI) is a Christian-based, non-profit NGO established in Liberia to serve communities through spiritual transformation, humanitarian outreach, and sustainable development.

We believe the Gospel restores the whole person — spirit, soul, and body. We partner with churches, government agencies, and international organisations to bring lasting change across Montserrado County.

What We Do

Our Ministries & Programmes

Every programme is rooted in Scripture and directed at meeting real needs in Liberian communities.

Church & Worship

Weekly worship services, prayer gatherings, and evangelistic outreach establishing prayer centres throughout Liberia.

Evangelism & Discipleship

Preaching the Gospel through conferences, seminars, and multimedia outreach across the nation of Liberia.

Education Support

Providing resources, materials, and mentorship to underserved children and youth across Montserrado County.

Youth & Women Empowerment

Promoting leadership, economic independence, and spiritual formation for young people and women.

Health Awareness

Community health campaigns and free screenings bringing care to families without access to healthcare.

Counselling & Spiritual Care

Spiritual guidance and pastoral counselling promoting healing, peace, and renewed purpose in Christ.
